Mental Health

Mental Health Tracking & Support Platform

We developed a comprehensive mental health application that helps users track their mood, offers guided meditation, and provides cognitive behavioral therapy exercises, all in a user-friendly interface.

The Challenge

Our client needed a solution that would make mental health support accessible to users anywhere, anytime, while maintaining clinical standards and patient privacy.

Our Solution

We created a HIPAA-compliant application with mood tracking, guided meditation sessions, CBT exercises, and a secure connection to therapists when needed.

The Results

  • 30% increase in user daily engagement
  • 85% of users reported improved well-being
  • Featured in top mental health resources
  • Seamless integration with telehealth services
