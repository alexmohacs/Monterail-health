Frequently Asked Questions

What healthcare regulations do you have experience with?

+

We have extensive experience working with various healthcare regulations including HIPAA, GDPR, FDA guidelines for software as a medical device (SaMD), ISO 13485, and other relevant standards. Our team stays up-to-date with evolving regulatory requirements to ensure that our healthcare solutions maintain compliance.

How long does a typical healthcare software project take?

+

The timeline for healthcare software projects varies significantly based on complexity, scope, and regulatory requirements. A minimum viable product (MVP) might take 3-6 months to develop, while a comprehensive enterprise healthcare solution could take 9-18 months or more. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific project requirements.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support services for healthcare applications. This includes regular updates, security patches, compliance monitoring, performance optimization, and technical support. We can tailor our support services to your specific needs and budget.

How do you handle data privacy and security in healthcare applications?

+

We implement comprehensive security measures including data encryption, access controls, audit logging, and vulnerability management. Our development process integrates security and privacy by design principles, and we conduct regular security assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities. All our healthcare solutions are built to comply with relevant privacy regulations like HIPAA and GDPR.

What technologies do you use for healthcare software development?

+

We use a variety of technologies for healthcare software development, including React and React Native for front-end, Ruby on Rails, Node.js, and Python for back-end, and various cloud platforms for hosting. We also work with healthcare-specific technologies such as HL7 FHIR for interoperability. Our technology choices are always guided by your specific project requirements, regulatory considerations, and best practices for healthcare software.

Do you have experience integrating with EHR/EMR systems?

+

Yes, we have experience integrating with various Electronic Health Record (EHR) and Electronic Medical Record (EMR) systems. We're familiar with healthcare interoperability standards like HL7 FHIR and can develop solutions that securely exchange data with existing healthcare systems, ensuring seamless workflows and data continuity.
